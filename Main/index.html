cultura contra balas

(CCB) is a non-profit organization that uses culture as a tool against violence. Through cultural and educational events, CCB unites Spain and countries affected by armed conflict.

ABOUT US
Who we are?

The NGO is a space for psychological rehabilitation and development. All of its projects are based on the work and support of dedicated volunteers. Led by a strong belief in humanity, we encourage everyone to spread kindness and inspire others!
