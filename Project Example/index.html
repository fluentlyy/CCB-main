RECOVERY AND THE POWER OF WOMEN

(CCB) is a non-profit organization that uses culture as a tool against violence. Through cultural and educational events, CCB unites Spain and countries affected by armed conflict.

Over two weeks, they learned to create social programs to help others. With special support from volunteers, they participated in karate classes and explored Javea. The local Rotary Club funded meals for the teens, and Borja, a Michelin-starred chef from the "Tula" restaurant, held a Spanish cuisine masterclass for them. An important part of the cultural exchange involved interactions with peers at two educational institutions, where they shared their experiences and future dreams.
